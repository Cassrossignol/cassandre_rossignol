"The world is a book and those who do not travel read only one page.” - Saint Augustine

I had the chance to live abroad for 12 years, 8 years in Poland and 4 years in China. These years of expatriation have allowed me to travel, to discover cultures, landscapes and splendid universes. They enrich me every day, they make my strength and have given me this desire for challenge and ambition.

Poland

I grew up in Poland, 6 years in Warsaw and 2 years in Krakow, from English school to French school, my character come from this beautiful country.

China

China, my most memorable trip, my college years in Beijing and Shang Hai have been so rich in experience, in emotion.

Australia

I have always lived my expatriate years with my family. Australia was the first long trip alone, with one of my best friends.

I discovered different stories, different cultures but also different ways of life. Abroad I learned to communicate in several languages, to open up to others and to listen. At each new destination, I had to adapt, integrate, rebuild a circle of friends, find a new balance and overcome difficulties. Each trip is a unique experience, they have the power to enrich my personality and my vision of the world. Recently, my trip to Australia confirmed my professional choices. During four months, I travelled, discovered a new culture in complete autonomy.From Sydney to Cairns we shared the best moments of our lives, as well as the most difficult ones.
